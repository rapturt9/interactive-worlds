# Webnovel Story Bible & Storyboard

## Purpose
Create a comprehensive story bible that serves as the foundation for your webnovel, including world-building, character development, plot structure, and pacing guidelines.

## Instructions

### 1. Basic Information
- **Title**: [Webnovel Title]
- **Genre**: [Primary and secondary genres]
- **Target Audience**: [Age range and demographics]
- **Planned Length**: [Number of chapters/arcs]
- **Update Schedule**: [How often new chapters will be released]

### 2. World-Building
- **Setting**: Time period, location, magic/tech systems
- **Rules & Constraints**: What's possible/impossible in this world
- **History**: Key historical events that shape the story
- **Social Structure**: Power dynamics, factions, organizations

### 3. Core Characters
For each major character:
- **Name & Role**:
- **Background**: Origin, motivations, fears
- **Arc**: How they'll develop throughout the story
- **Relationships**: Key connections with other characters
- **Voice**: How they speak and think

### 4. Plot Structure

#### Overall Arc
- **Premise**: Core concept in 2-3 sentences
- **Major Arcs**: Break story into 3-5 major arcs
- **Climactic Moments**: Key turning points

#### Arc Breakdown
For each arc:
- **Arc [Number]**: [Arc Name]
  - **Goal**: What this arc achieves
  - **Chapters**: [Start chapter - End chapter]
  - **Key Events**: Major plot points
  - **Character Development**: How characters change
  - **Hooks**: Cliffhangers and tension points

### 5. Pacing Guidelines

#### Chapter Structure
- **Average Chapter Length**: [Word count range]
- **Opening Hook**: How to start chapters
- **Closing Hook**: How to end chapters
- **Scene Balance**: Action vs. dialogue vs. introspection ratio

#### Tension Curve
- **Build-up Chapters**: [Number] chapters of rising tension
- **Climax Chapters**: [Number] chapters of peak intensity
- **Cooldown Chapters**: [Number] chapters of resolution
- **Pattern**: How to cycle through tension levels

### 6. Themes & Motifs
- **Central Themes**: What the story explores
- **Recurring Motifs**: Symbols, phrases, imagery
- **Emotional Tone**: Overall feel of the narrative

### 7. Serialization Strategy
- **Chapter Hooks**: Techniques to keep readers coming back
- **Arc Endings**: How to end each arc compellingly
- **Reader Engagement**: Mysteries, questions, speculation points
- **Payoff Schedule**: When to deliver on promises/setups

### 8. Continuity Notes
- **Timeline**: Key dates and chronology
- **Power Scaling**: How character abilities progress
- **World Details**: Important facts to keep consistent
- **Foreshadowing**: Planted seeds for future payoffs

## Output Format
Provide a detailed story bible following the structure above, tailored to your specific webnovel concept. Be thorough but flexible - this is a living document that will evolve.
