# Update Story Bible

## Purpose
Update and refine the story bible based on new developments, changes in direction, and lessons learned from writing chapters.

## When to Use This Template
- After completing a major arc
- When introducing significant new characters or plot elements
- When story direction shifts from original plan
- After reader feedback suggests adjustments
- Periodically (e.g., every 10 chapters) for maintenance

## Instructions

### 1. Review Current State
Assess what's been written:
- **Chapters Completed**: [Number]
- **Current Arc**: [Which arc are we in]
- **Recent Changes**: [Major developments since last update]
- **What Worked**: [Successful elements]
- **What Didn't**: [Elements that fell flat]

### 2. Continuity Audit
Check for consistency:
- **Timeline**: Are dates/ages still consistent?
- **Character Details**: Any contradictions in appearance, abilities, backstory?
- **World Rules**: Have magic/tech systems stayed consistent?
- **Geography**: Are locations and distances logical?
- **Power Levels**: Is character strength progression consistent?

### 3. Character Updates
For each major character:
- **Current State**: Where they are now
- **Development Progress**: How their arc has evolved
- **Relationship Changes**: New dynamics with other characters
- **Voice Refinement**: How their speaking style has crystallized
- **New Insights**: What you've learned about them while writing

### 4. Plot Adjustments
Update plot structure:
- **Completed Arcs**: Summarize what's been finished
- **Current Arc Revision**: Adjust based on how it's unfolding
- **Future Arc Updates**: Revise plans based on new directions
- **New Plot Threads**: Add storylines that emerged organically
- **Abandoned Threads**: Remove ideas that no longer fit

### 5. Pacing Refinement
Adjust pacing guidelines:
- **What's Working**: Pacing elements readers respond to
- **What Needs Adjustment**: Areas that feel too fast/slow
- **Chapter Length Revision**: Optimal word count based on feedback
- **Tension Pattern Updates**: Refined approach to builds/climaxes
- **Hook Strategy**: Refined cliffhanger techniques

### 6. World-Building Expansion
Add new world details:
- **New Locations**: Places introduced in recent chapters
- **New Rules**: World mechanics clarified through story
- **New Factions**: Organizations that have emerged
- **Historical Reveals**: Past events now known to readers
- **Cultural Details**: Customs, traditions, social norms established

### 7. Theme Development
Refine thematic elements:
- **Emerging Themes**: New ideas surfacing in the writing
- **Theme Expression**: How themes are manifesting in story
- **Motif Tracking**: Recurring symbols and imagery
- **Emotional Arc**: Overall tonal journey of the narrative

### 8. Serialization Strategy Update
Adjust engagement approach:
- **What Hooks Worked**: Most effective chapter endings
- **Reader Response**: Feedback on pacing and engagement
- **Mystery Management**: Which questions to answer when
- **Payoff Schedule**: Timing of reveals and resolutions
- **Arc Ending Strategy**: Refined approach to major conclusions

### 9. Foreshadowing & Setup Tracking
Manage future payoffs:
- **Seeds Planted**: Foreshadowing in recent chapters
- **Upcoming Payoffs**: Which setups are ready to resolve
- **Long-Term Setup**: Future story beats being prepared
- **Dropped Threads**: Elements that need revival or removal

### 10. Technical Notes
Document writing insights:
- **Chapter Templates**: Structures that work well
- **Scene Balance**: Effective ratios of action/dialogue/introspection
- **Writing Rhythm**: Optimal writing schedule/process
- **Research Needs**: Topics to research for future chapters
- **Style Notes**: Voice and prose techniques that work

## Update Process

### Step 1: Gather Information
- Review recent chapters
- Check reader feedback/comments
- Note any consistency issues
- Identify what worked and what didn't

### Step 2: Make Updates
- Revise affected sections of the story bible
- Add new information discovered through writing
- Remove or adjust elements that no longer fit
- Clarify anything that was vague

### Step 3: Future Planning
- Update arc plans for upcoming chapters
- Adjust pacing and structure as needed
- Plan new character developments
- Schedule future payoffs

### Step 4: Document Changes
- **Date of Update**: [Date]
- **Chapters Covered**: [Range]
- **Major Changes**: List key revisions
- **Reason for Changes**: Why adjustments were made
- **Impact on Future**: How this affects upcoming chapters

## Output Format
Provide:
1. Summary of changes made to the story bible
2. Updated sections (full revised text)
3. Notes on how changes impact future chapters
4. Action items for next writing session

## Continuity Checklist
- [ ] Timeline remains consistent
- [ ] Character details don't contradict earlier chapters
- [ ] World rules stay internally logical
- [ ] Power scaling makes sense
- [ ] Geography and distances are reasonable
- [ ] All plot threads are tracked
- [ ] Foreshadowing is documented
- [ ] Character arcs align with overall story

## Output
Provide the updated story bible sections with change notes and reasoning.
